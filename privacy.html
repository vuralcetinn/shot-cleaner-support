Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Shot Cleaner handles information when you use the app. Shot Cleaner is designed to help you review and clean your photo library on your device.

Photos and Photo Library Access

Shot Cleaner requests access to your photo library so you can review photos, keep photos, and remove photos you no longer want. Your photos are processed on your device for the app's cleanup and insight features.

Data We Store

Shot Cleaner may store app preferences, cleanup progress, kept photo references, statistics, and Pro insight cache data locally on your device. This helps the app remember your settings and show your cleanup progress.

Data We Do Not Sell

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising trackers in Shot Cleaner.

Purchases

Shot Cleaner uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases and subscriptions. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Third-Party Services

Shot Cleaner does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. Apple services may process information required for App Store purchases, subscriptions, crash diagnostics, or platform functionality according to Apple's policies.

Deleting Data

You can delete Shot Cleaner from your device to remove app data stored locally by the app. Photos deleted through the app are managed through Apple's Photos library behavior and may be recoverable for a limited time in the Photos app's Recently Deleted album.
